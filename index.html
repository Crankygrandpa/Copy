About

Hi! I am Thomas Szymankiewicz (I know, it looks really hard to say. It is, but don't worry about it.). I am a Freelance Copywriter based out of Dallas, Texas. I specialize in Product Descriptions, Content Writing, and Research/Analysis. I hold a Master's Degree in English from the University of Texas at Arlington, where I focused my studies on Literary Criticism and Theory as well as Rhetoric. My theoretical interests are primarily Marxist, Feminist, and Postcolonial theories. I am also a car and guitar nerd borderlining obsessed. I look forward to working with you on your next project!
